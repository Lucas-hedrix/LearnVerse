Who We Are

About Learn Verse Edu Hub

Learn Verse Edu Hub is a registered online brand with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria, dedicated to delivering exceptional, high-quality education for learners of all ages.

We specialise in personalised online learning experiences that empower children to build strong academic foundations while equipping adults with the skills needed to excel in languages and other essential areas.

Our commitment is to nurture confidence, inspire growth, and support every learner on their journey to success in a global and ever-evolving world.

Inclusive Learning

Special Education Needs

Every child deserves the chance to learn, grow, and thrive — regardless of how their brain is wired. At LearnVerse Hub, we offer compassionate, evidence-based support for children with special education needs, delivered by trained professionals in a safe and nurturing online environment.

Our SEN team works closely with families to understand each child's unique profile, designing personalised intervention plans that target specific areas of development. Whether your child needs communication support, help with daily living skills, or strategies to strengthen focus and memory, we meet them exactly where they are.

🗣️

Speech Therapy

Structured sessions targeting communication, language development, articulation, and social speech skills for children with delays or disorders.

✋

Occupational Therapy

Developing fine motor skills, sensory processing, and the everyday functional skills children need for learning and independence.

🧠

Cognitive Skills Development

Targeted exercises to strengthen memory, attention, executive function, and problem-solving capabilities in a supportive, encouraging setting.

📚

Learning Support

Specialised academic support for children with dyslexia, dyscalculia, ADHD, autism spectrum, and other learning differences.
